# ResilientPath: Official 2-Minute Demo Script

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Google Solution Challenge strictly enforces a 120-second limit for demo videos. You must practice this script with a stopwatch. Speak clearly, energetically, and follow the visual actions exactly.

---

### [0:00 - 0:20] THE PROBLEM & INTRODUCTION (20 Seconds)
**Visual:** Start with the camera facing you (the presenter).
**Audio:** "Hi, we are Team [Your Team Name], and we are tackling the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 9 and 11."
**Visual:** Cut to a screen recording of your mobile phone. Show a photo of a terrible pothole or broken road on your screen.
**Audio:** "Worldwide, cities waste billions of dollars repeatedly fixing the exact same potholes because corrupt contractors cut corners on materials. We built ResilientPath AI to completely automate infrastructure repair and eliminate corruption."

### [0:20 - 0:50] TECHNOLOGY: TF.JS EDGE SCANNER (30 Seconds)
**Visual:** On your mobile phone, open the ResilientPath app. Tap "Start" and point the camera at a picture of a pothole (on your laptop screen) or a real one. 
**Audio:** "This is our mobile scanner. It uses **TensorFlow.js** to run a custom MobileNet model entirely on-device. There is zero server cost, meaning municipal garbage trucks or public buses can mount this and scan city roads offline, in real-time."
**Visual:** The app detects "POTHOLE" and logs a ticket. Show the ticket appearing.

### [0:50 - 1:15] TECHNOLOGY: GEMINI FRAUD DETECTION (25 Seconds)
**Visual:** Cut to the Desktop Dashboard. Click the "VERIFY" tab.
**Audio:** "When a ticket syncs to Firebase, our system triggers the **Google Gemini API**. Gemini pulls the live satellite imagery from the exact GPS coordinates and cross-references it with the camera photo to mathematically verify that the damage exists. This prevents contractors from submitting fake photos to steal repair funds."
**Visual:** Show Gemini analyzing the satellite image and giving the "VERIFIED" score.

### [1:15 - 1:50] INNOVATION: CIVIC-YIELD SIMULATOR (35 Seconds)
**Visual:** Scroll down the dashboard to the "Civic-Yield Simulator" graph (the chart with red and green lines). Highlight it with your mouse.
**Audio:** "To solve SDG 16 (Strong Institutions), we built a trustless economic model. Instead of paying contractors upfront, our system automatically escrows 15% of their payment. As shown in our Civic-Yield Simulator, if the road breaks within 12 months, the smart contract zeros out their payout and forces a rework. If it holds, they get their bonus. We force them to build quality infrastructure."

### [1:50 - 2:00] CONCLUSION (10 Seconds)
**Visual:** Cut back to your face or the team logo.
**Audio:** "By combining TensorFlow, Gemini, and Firebase, ResilientPath doesn't just fix roads—it fixes the system that breaks them. Thank you."

---

## Recording Tips
*   **Do not speed-read:** If you talk too fast, the judges won't understand the technology.
*   **Show, don't just tell:** Make sure the TensorFlow scanner identifying the pothole takes up the entire screen when you talk about it.
*   **Use Captions:** Add English subtitles to your final video to ensure the global judging panel can follow along perfectly.
